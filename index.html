Korea (한국)

General Info

South Korea is a country in east asia. It is the southern part of the Korean peninsula.
The country is bordered by North Korea and Japan. The population is around 52 million people.
The capital of South Korea is Seoul.
The general geography of South Korea is mountainous.
Most of the population is native Korean, with less than 3% of the population being immigrants.
South Korea is also very serious about education, with them being in the top 10 globally.
South Korea has a lot to offer, such as culture, food, history, etc.
